# SmartDocs Library - Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 12, 2026

**SmartDocs Library** respects your privacy. The app is designed to work primarily on your device and, by default, does not collect personal information from you within the app.

## Information We Process

SmartDocs Library does not use advertising SDKs, third-party analytics tools, third-party tracking technologies, or in-app user accounts.

The documents, scans, filenames, tags, notes, extracted text, dates, folder structure, favorites, pinned items, and other library metadata you create or import are stored by you on your device.

If you choose to enable iCloud synchronization, your document metadata and app data may also be stored in and synchronized through your personal iCloud account using Apple’s iCloud services.

SmartDocs Library does not access your contacts, photos, or other device data except as needed for features you explicitly choose to use, such as importing a file, scanning a document, or selecting an image or document from a system picker.

## Data Storage

SmartDocs Library does not operate its own servers for storing your document library and does not store your document content on developer-controlled servers.

If you enable iCloud synchronization, eligible app data is also stored in your iCloud account and synced by Apple. iCloud sync is optional and can be enabled or disabled at any time. Apple manages iCloud data according to its own terms and privacy policies.



## Data Sharing

SmartDocs Library does not sell your personal information and does not share your document data with third parties for advertising, marketing, or analytics purposes.

Your data may be processed by Apple to provide system features you choose to use, such as iCloud synchronization, document import and export, file storage, or document scanning.

## User Control

You control your data within the app. You can edit or delete documents, metadata, tags, and folders at any time.

Deleting the app removes local app data from your device, but it may not immediately remove data stored in iCloud. Data stored in iCloud can be managed or deleted through your Apple account and iCloud settings.

## Contacting Us

If you contact us for support or privacy questions, we may receive your email address and any information you include in your message. We use that information only to respond to you and handle your request.

## Data Retention

Because SmartDocs Library is designed to store data locally and optionally in your iCloud account, you generally control how long your document data is retained.

If you contact us by email, we may retain your message and contact information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request, follow up on the issue, or comply with legal obligations.

## Children’s Privacy

SmartDocs Library is not directed to children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children within the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time if the app or our privacy practices change. Any updates will be reflected on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised.
