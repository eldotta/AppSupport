# SmartDocs Library
Your smart document helper.

SmartDocs is a beautifully simple document library for iCloud Drive that helps you capture, organize, find, and manage important files without friction. Scan paper documents directly in the app, import files through a dedicated Inbox, share from other apps and turn everything into a clean, searchable library organized the way you work.

SmartDocs combines fast document intake with intelligent text extraction, date detection, rich metadata, tags, favorites, and pinned items. Every document can be reviewed, titled, dated, tagged, and filed into the right folder before it becomes part of your permanent library.

SmartDocs keeps your library structured and safe. Documents are stored in folders instead of cluttering the root, deleted items move to Trash for recovery, and metadata stays synced across devices through iCloud-powered app sync. Even large libraries remain easy to browse with hierarchical folders, mixed folder-and-document views, previews for supported file types, and full-text search across filenames, tags, metadata, and extracted text.

Whether you’re scanning receipts, saving records, or maintaining a personal archive, SmartDocs gives you a polished, privacy-conscious document workflow built around iCloud Drive and native iOS experiences.

Key benefits
	•	Scan, import, and process documents in one streamlined flow
	•	Organize files with folders, tags, favorites, and pinned items
	•	Search across document text, filenames, and metadata
	•	Preview and edit documents with a shared, consistent editor
	•	Recover deleted files from Trash before permanent removal
	•	Sync document metadata and app state across devices
	•	Work naturally with iCloud Drive using familiar Apple-style interactions
